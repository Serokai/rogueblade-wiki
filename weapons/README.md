# Weapons

Weapons are the main way you deal damage. They scale with different stats depending on their type.

* **Swords** — melee, scale with Strength
* **Staffs** — magic, scale with Intelligence, consume Mana
* **Books** — support / magic, scale with Faith or Intelligence
* **Slingshots & Bows** — ranged, scale with Ranged damage
* **Guns** — ranged, scale with Ranged damage
* **Shields** — defensive, used to block and reduce damage
