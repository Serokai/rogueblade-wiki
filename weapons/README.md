# ⚔️ Weapons

Weapons are your primary source of damage. They scale with different stats depending on their type.

| Type | Scales with | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 🗡️ [**Swords**](swords.md) | Strength | Melee, fastest attack speed, no resource cost |
| 🔮 [**Staffs**](staffs.md) | Intelligence | Magic, consume Mana, AoE or utility |
| 📖 [**Books**](books.md) | Faith (usually) | Support / healing / summon - often team-wide effects |
| 🏹 [**Slingshots & Bows**](slingshots-bows.md) | Ranged | Projectile weapons, safer kiting |
| 🛡️ [**Shields**](shields.md) | Defense | Block to reduce damage; some grant passive buffs |

{% hint style="info" %}
Some class-specific weapons don't fit neatly into these buckets - Engineer's wrench, Cowboy's revolver, Druid's plant totems, Pumpkin's rocket launcher. Those are documented on the individual class pages.
{% endhint %}

## 🎨 Rarity & biomes

Some weapons are biome-exclusive and only drop from specific enemies or appear in specific shops:

* 🌿 **Jungle** - Venomshank
* ❄️ **Gelo / Ice** - Ice Dagger
* 🔥 **Moltenica** - Crimtane Blade, Fire Sword
* 🧟 **Necrotic Graves** - Necrotic Staff, Skeleton weapons
