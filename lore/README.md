# 📖 Lore

RogueBlade has more lore than it lets on: rival gods, demon politics, mushroom civilisations, and a merchant with suspicious prices.

* ✨ [**Gods**](gods.md) - Marble King, Dragonslayer Tug, Bed of Death, Pyromancer of Moltenica, Liontamer Jiben
* 👹 [**Demons (race)**](demons.md) - infernal faction and their champions
* 🍄 [**Mush (race)**](mush.md) - mushroom folk of the Highlands
* 💰 [**Lucy**](lucy.md) - the lobby merchant NPC

{% hint style="info" %}
Most lore is implied through boss dialogue, item descriptions and class backstory rather than explicit quests. Cross-references to the patch notes and the official Fandom wiki are noted on each page.
{% endhint %}
